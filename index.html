Color and Vision Network Community Calendar

This is the initial version of a dynamic schedule that will be updated regularly with the latest available information. Conferences and other items are listed in chronological order, though deadlines (e.g., for abstracts) are not. This list also includes job openings, postdoctoral fellowships, student positions, online talks, and other opportunities relevant to the color and vision research community. Eventually, this list will be posted on CVnet’s forthcoming website, cvnet.org

If you have suggestions for additions or changes, please send them directly to David Peterzell and Michaela Tedesco at:
davidpeterzell@mac.com
mtedesco12@gmail.com

Please notify us even if you’ve already shared this information via CVnet listserv.

In organizing this schedule, we try to keep up with CVnet postings, but notes to us are helpful in maintaining up-to-date information. Additional opportunities may be found by visiting the CVnet and VisionList archives:
