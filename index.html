Passings

Tuesday, 18 March 2026

Dear CVNet colleagues,

Two years ago, during a discussion on CVNet about which deceased vision scientists should be remembered and documented, Lothar Spillmann undertook the ambitious task of compiling a near-comprehensive list. Over the intervening period, he and several colleagues have worked extensively to assemble a remarkable resource: a list of deceased vision scientists accompanied by brief biographical notes.

We are very pleased to make this work available to the community. The document is now available on the CVNet website as a PDF, exactly as submitted to us. The PDF can be downloaded here.

Soon, we will also release an online version of the list. This version will allow entries to be indexed and browsed in multiple ways—for example, by alphabetical order, year of birth, year of death, and other useful categories, in addition to the chapters included in Lothar’s original list—making it easier to explore the history of our field.

If you notice errors or omissions, or have suggestions for additions or corrections, please feel free to send them to Christopher Tyler and David Peterzell, who are coordinating CVNet updates for this project. This is intended to be a living document that will be refined and expanded over time.

We are grateful to Lothar and his collaborators for the considerable effort that went into assembling this valuable historical resource.

Best regards,
David Peterzell
(for CVNet)
